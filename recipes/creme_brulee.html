Crème Brûlée Recipe

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream: 4 Cups
  • Vanilla Bean: 1, Split and Scraped
  • Vanilla Sugar: 1 Cup
  • Large Eggs: 6
  • Hot Water: 8 Cups

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Put cream, vanilla bean and its pulp, into a medium pan and bring it to a boil over medium high height. When boiling, remove, cover, and let it sit for 15 min.
  3. In a bowl, whisk 1/2 cup of sugar and the egg yolks until well blended, and colour starts to lighten in colour. Add the cream a little at a time, while stirring between pours. Pour liquid into 6 different ramekins. Place those ramekins on a cake pan. Pour enough hot water into the cake pan so it ocvers hald of the ramekins, and bake until the creme brulee is set but still trembling. Approximately 40-45 minutes
  4. Remove ramekins and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, and up to 3 days. Remove from fridge for at least 30 min before browning the sugar of top. Divade the extra 1/2 cup of sugar equally onto the 6 dishes, and while carefully using a torch, melt the sugar for a crispy top.
  5. Let ut sit for 5 minutes, and enjoy!

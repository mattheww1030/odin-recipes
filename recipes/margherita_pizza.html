Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough: 1 12-inch, stretched
  • Tomato Sauce: 3 Tablespoons
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Mozzarella: 2 3/4 Ounces
  • Basil: 4-5 Leaves

Directions

  1. Place pizza stone on the middle rack of the oven, and turn heat to the highest setting. Allow it to heat for at least an hour.
  2. Put sauce in the middle of the stretched dough, and spread evenly. Stop spreading when sauce is approximately 1/2 inch away from edges.
  3. Drizzle a little olive oil on the pizza. Break cheese into large chunks and gently place on the sauce. Scatter basil leaves on top.
  4. PLace pizza in oven, and bake until crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling. About 4-8 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!

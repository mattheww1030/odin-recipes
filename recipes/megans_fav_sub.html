The Worst Possible Sub's Recipe

Ingredients

  • Italian herbs and cheese bread: 1 6 Inch Slice
  • Turkey slices: 6 Slices
  • White cheese: 3 Slices
  • Iceberg Lettuce: 4 Lettuce Leaves
  • Cucumber: 8 Slices
  • Green bell pepper: 1/2 of Pepper, Sliced
  • Green olives: 4 olives, sliced

Directions

  1. If wanted toasted, which is recommended since it enhances the taste, preheat oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Slice bread in half horizantally, and place all remaining indredients on the bread
  3. If not toasting, then you're all done! Enjoy your cold sub!!!!! If not, place sub in oven and toast until bread in golden brown. Approximately 3 minutes
  4. Take out of oven and enjoy!

Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • Water: 4 Cups
  • White Rice: 2 Cups
  • Boneless Chicken Breast, skinless and thinly sliced: 3 Halves
  • Soy Sauce: 2/3 Cup
  • Brown Sugar: 1/4 Cup
  • Cornstarch: 1 Tablespoon
  • Sesame Oil: 2 Tablespoons
  • *Minced Fresh Ginger: 1 Tablespoon
  • *Minced Garlic: 1 Tablespoon
  • *Red Pepper Flakes: 1/4 Teaspoon
  • *Broccoli Head: 1
  • *Onion, sliced in large chunks: 1
  • *Carrots, sliced: 1 Cup
  • *Water Chestnuts, sliced: 1 Can
  • *Green Bell Pepper, sliced: 1

*Not neccesary, but sufficient. Add depending what you want

Directions

  1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a pan on high heat. After water starts to boil, reduce heat to medium, cover, and simmer till rice is tender and the water is gone. 20-25 min.
  2. While waiting for rice to finish, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, and cornstarch in a bowl and stir till smooth. Stir in ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes if wanted, as well as chicken, and stir till coated. Cover and marinate in fridge for a minimum of 15 min.
  3. Heat pan on medium heat and grease with sesame oil. Add optional vegetables and cook until tender, for about 5 min. Set aside onto a dish.
  4. Afterwards, add in chicken without the marinade, and cook until browned. Finally, add in marinade and the cooked vegetables, and cook till chicken is completely cooked, 5-7 min.
  5. Dish a plate with white rice, and add a portion of the pan mixture on top of the rice. Enjoy!
